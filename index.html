Our Story

Scrappy Products is a division of Banner Creations, Inc. BCI is an eco-savvy woman-owned custom manufacturer of banners, flags, table covers, street banners, fabric walls and much more. Since our beginning in 1989 we have felt a responsibility to manage our environmental impact. We have always recycled our paper, used water-based inks and been pioneers in steering the industry toward green processes and products. Scrappy Products was born out of that sense of responsibility toward our environment. Our bags are made from a collection of scrap banners, misprints, overprints and fabric scraps. The majority are made from 100% upcycled fabric made from recycled water bottles. They are recyclable, washable, very durable and printed with water-based inks. When you're finally done with your bags your great-grandchildren can send them back to the mill to be upcycled again. You may also be interested to know that it takes approximately 10 plastic water bottles to make 1 tote. All bags can be custom printed with a minimum quantity of 50.
